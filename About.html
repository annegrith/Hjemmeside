Personal background.


After years of teaching communication at an analysis institute, I’ve started studying multimedia design at UCL in Odense, Denmark. As creativity has always been a large part of my life, both presenting itself as paintings, design and writing, I have found my personal style of minimalism. I strive to stay updated and educate myself further, whilst constantly gathering the latest development within the field of graphic design.
